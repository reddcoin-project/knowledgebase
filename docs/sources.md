This knowledgebase is assembled from a mix of:

- **Canonical / project-controlled sources** (preferred)
- **Operational sources** (explorers/APIs that reflect live chain state)
- **Community-maintained sources** (useful, but may be dated)
- **Market-data aggregators** (useful, but treat as provider-dependent)
