# Exchange history timeline (high-level)

This is a **history/ops** view of how RDD access has evolved across market cycles.

## 2014–2017: Expansion era
- Early exchange growth alongside broader altcoin adoption.
- Community tipping culture develops and spreads.

## 2018: Peak coverage, early contractions
- Many “first generation” assets see rotation and liquidity fragmentation.

## 2020–2021: Listing outreach + offers
- ReddHeads ran a community exchange crowdfund.
- Public offers and discussions occurred with providers like Bitcoin.com / exchange.bitcoin.com.

Sources:
- Exchange crowdfund (ProjectRedd Medium): https://medium.com/projectredd/reddhead-community-outreach-reddcoin-rdd-exchange-crowdfund-2020-cc65c0f7c55b
- Q1 2021 dev update (Bitcoin.com offer mention): https://medium.com/projectredd/3-26-21-end-q1-reddcoin-rdd-core-dev-status-update-9fbc075d427f

## 2022+: Community trading phase
- Market access may be primarily via smaller exchanges.
- Always verify deposits/withdrawals and self‑custody immediately after trading.
