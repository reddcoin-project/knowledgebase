# What is ReddCoin?

ReddCoin (ticker: **RDD**, symbol: **Ɍ**) is a peer-to-peer open-source cryptocurrency focused on **social tipping and small payments**.

## Purpose

ReddCoin’s long-running theme is simple: turn “likes” and other social engagement into **real, spendable value** via tipping and micropayments.

## Key facts (high level)

- Launched in **2014** as a fork of Litecoin
- Runs on its own proof-of-stake family consensus (PoSV / PoSV v2)
- Designed to be lightweight and usable for everyday social payments
