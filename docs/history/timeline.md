## Early era (high-level)

- **Jan–Feb 2014** — ReddCoin introduced and launched (early PoW/PoS-era altcoin wave).
- **Aug 2014 (approx.)** — Mining era winds down; ReddCoin operates under Proof-of-Stake Velocity (PoSV).

> Some early-era dates still need primary citations in this wiki (coingecko / historical announcements / repo tags).

## PoSV v2 / Enhanced Staking era (design milestone)

- **Summer 2019** — Updated ReddPaper / Roadmap describing **PoSV v2** and Enhanced Staking released (see ReddPaper/ReddBook sources).

## Recent operational milestones (from Telegram support)

- **2024-01-19** — Core **v4.22.8** release announced in Telegram (included a fix for stalling downloads).[^message1975134]
- **2024-08-17** — Advisory: legacy nodes/services urged to upgrade due to chain split risk across mixed versions.[^message2000580]
- **2024-09-14** — Admin published hash-check procedure for two known “bad forks” (heights + hashes + invalidate commands).[^message2002551]
- **2025-12-15** — Dev update: v4.22.10 in progress (SegWit/CSV/Taproot roadmap; testing focus).[^message2036694]
- **2026-02-12** — Admin reiterated canonical hash check at height 5,519,068.[^message2039543]
