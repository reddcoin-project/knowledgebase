# PoSV v2 paper

**Title:** *Proof-of-Stake-Velocity v2 (PoSV v2) — Enhancing the social currency of the digital age*  
**Date:** August 2019  
**Authors:** Jay “TechAdept” Laurence & John “Crypto Gnasher” Nash

PDF: [ReddPaper-PoSV-v2-Summer-2019.pdf](../assets/docs/ReddPaper-PoSV-v2-Summer-2019.pdf)

## Scope

This document is the deep-dive specification and motivation for PoSV v2.
It is the best single source for:

- why PoSV v1 needed enhancement
- how PoSV v2 improves incentives and long-term stability
- how PoSV v2 targets a 5% network inflation goal (via adjustment)
- operational expectations for stakers and node operators
