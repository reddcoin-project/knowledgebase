# ReddPaper

**Title:** *REDD PAPER — A look into our technology*  
**Date:** Aug 2019  
**Authors:** Jay “TechAdept” Laurence & John “Gnasher” Nash  

PDF: [Redd-Paper.pdf](https://redd.love/assets/doc/Redd-Paper.pdf)

## What it contains

The ReddPaper is the project’s updated whitepaper (originally April 2014), covering:

- Proof of Work and Proof of Stake history in ReddCoin
- Proof of Stake Velocity (PoSV) design + motivations
- Monetary concepts (medium of exchange, store of value, inflation/deflation)
- ReddCoin’s position as a "digital social currency" and tipping primitive
